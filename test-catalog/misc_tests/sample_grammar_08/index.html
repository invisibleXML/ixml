sample.grammar.08

Created 08 Feb 2022 by cmsmcq

Trivial right-recursive grammar, from Loup Vaillant and Grune/Jacobs. Unless they use Leo's optimization (or some other clever technique I haven't heard of), Earley parsers will take quadratic time and space on this.

L(G) = {'', 'a', 'aa', 'aaa', ... } = regex a*.
